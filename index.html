📜 KBC Event – Rules & Guidelines

  • 1. Participation
    • Participants can compete individually or in a team of two.
    • All participants must register before the event begins.
    • Team members cannot be changed once registered.
  • 2. Fastest Finger First (FFF) Round
    • All participants will play the Fastest Finger First round.
    • The participant who gives the correct answer in the shortest time will reach the Hot Seat.
    • After one contestant completes the Hot Seat round, the process will restart with Fastest Finger First to select the next contestant.
  • 3. Hot Seat Round
    • The selected participant will answer General Knowledge (GK) questions.
    • Each question will have four options (A, B, C, D).
    • The difficulty level of the questions will gradually increase.
    • A timer will be displayed for each question.
    • Contestants must clearly state their final answer before the timer runs out.
  • 4. Lifelines
    • Each contestant/team can use three lifelines (only once each):
    • Audience Poll – Audience votes for the answer.
    • 50:50 – Two incorrect options are removed.
    • Swap the Question – The current question is replaced with a new one.
  • 5. Determining the Winner
    • At the end of the event, only ONE WINNER will be declared based on:
    • Maximum number of correct answers and points accumulated.
    • Least number of lifelines used.
    • Minimum time taken.
    • If required, a tie-breaker question will be asked.
  • 6. Fair Play
    • Contestants must submit their phones before sitting on the Hot Seat.
    • Audience members must not reveal or signal answers to the contestant.
    • Any form of cheating or disturbing contestants will result in immediate disqualification.
    • While a contestant is answering, others must remain silent.
  • 7. Final Authority
    • The decision of the host and organizing committee will be final and cannot be challenged.
